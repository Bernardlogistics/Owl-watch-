# # 👁️ Owl Watch — Forensic Procurement Intelligence Platform

An interactive, executive-level analytics dashboard designed to identify contract non-compliance, supply chain leakage, and immediately recoverable spend. This platform serves as a "CFO Command View," transforming raw supply chain transactional data into actionable financial oversight.

## 📊 Core Analytical Capabilities

* **Invoice & Freight Auditing:** Flags contract rate variances across specific freight lanes and isolates fuel surcharge discrepancies.
* **Bill of Materials (BOM) Drift Tracking:** Monitors real-time delivered raw material and component costs against pre-negotiated contract benchmarks per unit.
* **Vendor Risk Architecture:** Dynamically ranks and flags high-risk vendors using a combined matrix of price drift percentage, variance events, and operational delivery failures.
* **Lead-Time Milestone Matrix:** Measures actual vs. expected purchase order (PO) transit timelines and quantifies the exact financial impact of supply chain delays (factored at a $250/day line-stoppage risk cost).

## 🛠️ Behind the System
While the frontend provides a clean, web-based executive viewport, the underlying engine is powered by a robust, multi-sheet analytical logic model. It mimics a pipeline that ingests historical procurement ledger exports, automatically screens for contract discrepancies, and visualizes immediate cost-recovery opportunities.
