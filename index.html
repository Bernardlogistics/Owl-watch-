About this demo. Owl Watch is a forensic procurement intelligence workbook — invoice & freight auditing, BOM drift tracking, vendor risk scoring, and freight lane benchmarking, all built on live Excel formulas. Every figure on this page is fictional sample data generated to illustrate how the tool flags variance, risk, and recoverable spend. The underlying engine is a working Excel model with linked formulas across eight sheets, available as a companion download.

Design and build: Pedro B., Supply Chain & Procurement.
